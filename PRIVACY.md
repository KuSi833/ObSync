# Privacy Policy

**ObSync** does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Authentication

ObSync uses GitHub OAuth to authenticate. Your GitHub access token is stored locally on your device in the iOS Keychain. It is never sent to any server other than GitHub's API.

## Data Storage

All data (vault configurations, folder bookmarks) is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any third-party service.

## Analytics

ObSync does not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/KuSi833/ObSync/issues.
